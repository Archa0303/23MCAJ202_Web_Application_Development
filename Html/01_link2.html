Explore India's Top Destinations

Taj Mahal

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The white marble masterpiece is located in Agra and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Jaipur

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its grand forts and palaces. Key attractions include Amer Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. The city is also known for its vibrant markets and Rajasthani cuisine.

Goa

A paradise for beach lovers, Goa offers golden sands, thrilling nightlife, and delicious seafood. The influence of Portuguese culture is evident in its churches and architecture.

Manali

A beautiful hill station surrounded by snowy mountains and lush valleys, Manali is perfect for adventure seekers. Activities like paragliding, trekking, and river rafting attract tourists from all over.
